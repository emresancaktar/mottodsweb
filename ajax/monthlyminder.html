MonthlyMinder V1.1

Finans · Temmuz 2014 · Apple Store

Monthly Minder is an advanced system to track monthly payments, and bills. It developed based on Turkey’s payment norms. It designed to ask for minimum number of data, and give back maximum details and data. The interface we used is offering you to add payments under 3 seconds. Amongst the similar applications, this is the easiest, the purest, and the fastest one. You need to add your usual payments to the application only for once. Unlike other applications, Monthly Minder doesn’t want you to add neither your bills monthly, nor your every payments. That’s why it doesn’t cause you any trouble to adding unnecessary data, and in return it offers what you need. It’s duty is not get information from you, but to give it.
